拔牙暴龍 / Extractosaurus



Background Story

Extractosaurus is a dentist known for his... unique approach to tooth extraction.

With his powerful arms and questionable techniques, teeth tend to "just come off" during his procedures.

Whether this is due to his natural Extracrosaurus strength or his dental expertise remains a topic of debate.

Character Profile

  • Chinese Name: 拔牙暴龍
  • English Name: Extractosaurus
  • Specialty: Tooth Extraction
  • Catchphrase: "It just came off!"
