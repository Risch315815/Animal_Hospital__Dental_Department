洗牙貓貓 / Scaling Kitty



Background Story

Scaling Kitty is a periodontal specialist who takes great pride in his minimally invasive procedures and up-to-date knowledge.

He hates Extractosaurus for his excessively aggressive approach, and the collateral damage he causes.

They have been frenemies for forever.
