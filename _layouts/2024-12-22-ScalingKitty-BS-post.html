洗牙貓貓 / Scaling Kitty



Background Story

Scaling Kitty is a periodontal specialist who takes great pride in his minimally invasive procedures and staying up-to-date with the latest guidelines.

He hates Extractosaurus for his excessively aggressive approach, and the collateral damage he causes.

However, for some reason, they have been keeping close company thus far.

Character Profile
